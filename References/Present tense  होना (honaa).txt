Present tense : होना (honaa)

Past Tense : हुआ (huaa)

Future : होगा (hogaa)

Meaning of what happened in Hindi
क्या होता है ? (kyaa hotaa hai) = What happens ?

क्या हो रहा है ? (kyaa ho rahaa hai)  = What is happening ?

मुझे कुछ हो रहा है | (mujhe kuchh ho rahaa hai) = Something is happening to me.

पीटर को कुछ हो रहा है | (peter ko  kuchh ho rahaa hai) = Something is happening to Peter.

क्या हुआ ? (kyaa huaa ) = What happened ?
कल क्या हुआ ? (kal kyaa huaa) = What happened yesterday ?

मैच में क्या हुआ ? (match me kyaa hua) = What happened in the match ?

ऑफिस में क्या हुआ ? (office me kyaa huaa) = What happened in the office ?

क्या होगा ? (kyaa hogaa ?) = What will happen ?

अब क्या होगा ? (ab kyaa hogaa) = What will happen now ?

कल क्या होगा ? (kal kyaa hogaa) = What will happen tomorrow ?
